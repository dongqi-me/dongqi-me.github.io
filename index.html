I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate (doctoral researcher) funded by the European Research Council, affiliated with the project: Individualized Interaction in Discourse, led by Prof. Dr. Vera Demberg at Saarland Informatics Campus, Saarland University. I was also a visiting Ph.D. student at the University of Edinburgh from February 2024 to August 2024, working under the guidance of Prof. Dr. Mirella Lapata. To contribute to the NLP community, I joined the ACL Rolling Review Team as a technical support member, primarily responsible for ARR website updates.

Prior to joining Saarland University, I earned my Master of Computer Science degree under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Khalil Sima'an at the Institute for Logic, Language and Computation, University of Amsterdam.
